# Privacy Policy 

This application does not collect or process any personal data from users.  
The app is designed solely to provide informational content in the form of assembly guides for 3D printable model cars. No login or personal data input is required.

During the use of the app, files may be downloaded from external content delivery services such as Cloudflare. These services may collect anonymous technical data (such as IP address or network information) necessary to deliver the content.  
Such data is not accessible to or controlled by the app developer.

The app does not share personal data with third parties and does not use tracking or analytics tools.
